About Truthpedia

What is Truthpedia?
Truthpedia is a decentralized, fully anonymous platform where truth has no barriers. Expose corruption. Report injustice. Reveal wrongdoing safely, without compromise, and without a trace. Our team, independent journalists, and the general public work together to uncover what the powerful want hidden.
Who runs Truthpedia?
Truthpedia is founded and led by a young visionary committed to truth and transparency. The core team and contributors operate anonymously to maintain security and independence.
Is Truthpedia affiliated with any government, corporation, or political group?
No. Truthpedia is completely independent. We have no ties to governments, corporations, political parties, or intelligence agencies.
How is Truthpedia different from WikiLeaks or other leak platforms?
Let's be clear, Truthpedia is not a leak platform. Where raw files are dumped and people are left to sift through thousands of documents, we do the hard work for you. Our team, independent journalists, and contributors investigate, verify, and distill the truth into concise, compelling stories, delivered in video format or as sharp, digestible reads. No jargon. No overwhelm. Just the truth, presented clearly.

Legal & Privacy

Is Truthpedia Legal?
Operating and using Truthpedia is legal in most jurisdictions. The risk is ours, not yours. Our architecture is built so that users are never exposed. You receive the truth safely, anonymously, and without consequence. There are no accounts, no logs, and no traces—nothing that could ever connect you to what you read or share. Whatever legal, political, or institutional pressure may come, it lands on us, not on you.
How Anonymous Is Truthpedia?
Completely. No one who uses this platform is ever exposed. No accounts. No emails. No IP logging. No metadata. No fingerprints, digital or otherwise. Whether you are a whistleblower risking exposing injustice, a journalist protecting a source, or simply a person seeking the truth, your identity is untouchable here.

Submissions & Publishing

How do I submit a story or tip to Truthpedia?
Simple. If you have information, evidence, or a story that exposes corruption, injustice, or wrongdoing, we want to hear it. Hit the "Contact Us" button in the menu, tell us what you know, and share whatever proof or documentation you have behind it. Our team will review it, verify it, and if credible, transform it into a concise, compelling story delivered to the world.
What counts as a story worth submitting?
If it's true, provable, and the public deserves to know.
Do I need to reveal who I am?
Never. We ask only for the truth and the evidence behind it, nothing that could ever identify you.
What kind of proof do I need?
Documents, recordings, screenshots, videos, testimonies—anything that substantiates your claim. The stronger the evidence, the faster we can act. You have the truth. We have the platform. And we'll expose it together.
Will my submission be published automatically?
No. All submissions are reviewed by our team for authenticity, significance, and safety before any public release.
Can I stay in contact after submitting?
Absolutely, and entirely on your terms. You can reach us anytime via our Gmail, our privacy-focused email addresses, or on Telegram. You're also welcome to provide your own secure communication method—a temporary encrypted email, a private key, or whatever you're most comfortable with. We will never ask for, store, or retain any personal information, ever.
How do you verify submissions?
Our team of experienced journalists and researchers carefully examines documents, cross-references claims, and assesses credibility before publishing.
Who decides what gets published?
The founder and an independent editorial team operating anonymously, guided by journalistic standards and our mission.
Will you publish everything you receive?
No. We publish only credible, significant information that serves the public interest.

Risks & Safety

What precautions should I take before submitting?
None. You don't submit anything directly to the platform. You simply reach out to us via Gmail, our privacy-focused email addresses, or Telegram, and tell us what you have. We take it from there. We are the ones who handle, verify, and publish.
Can Truthpedia Be Shut Down?
No. And here's why:
  • Fully decentralized: There is no central server to seize, no single point to target.
  • No registered entity: We have no legal address, no company, no jurisdiction that owns us.
  • No data to confiscate: We store nothing, so there is nothing to hand over.
  • No identities to expose: Neither ours nor yours.
  • No single team: Our network of contributors spans the globe and cannot be dismantled in one move.
Built to survive pressure. No court order, government, or institution can shut down what they cannot find.
